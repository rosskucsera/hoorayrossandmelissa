ABOUT US

ROSS

Ross spends his days producing graphics and webpages for software company PeopleMatter in Charleston. He was born in Scotland, grew up a military brat and graduated from Clemson University, where he perfected the art of the dining hall breakfast. When not pushing pixels at work, Ross prefers exploring new places with his favorite travel buddy, Melissa.

MELISSA

Melissa is a certified physician assistant who moved to Charleston after being the fourth Whitson to graduate from Clemson University. She's a newly discovered morning person who has a penchant for neon colored running shoes and hot air balloons. Her ideal Saturday involves coffee, thrift shops, and Thai food with her favorite adventure partner, Ross.

With a shared love for writing, design, and working late into the night, Ross and Melissa both joined the staff of Clemson's newspaper, The Tiger. Though they worked for the paper as freshmen, the couple did not meet until their sophomore year when Melissa was elected news editor with the help of Ross' history-making vote.
