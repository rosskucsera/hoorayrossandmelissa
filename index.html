About us

Ross

Ross spends his days producing art for software company PeopleMatter in Charleston. When not pushing pixels at work, Ross prefers exploring new places with his favorite travel buddy, Melissa.

Melissa

Her ideal Saturday involves coffee, thrift shops, and Thai food with her favorite adventure partner, Ross.
